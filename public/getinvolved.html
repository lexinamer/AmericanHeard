Get Involved

America Heard is comprised of collaborators who freely give their time and talents to contribute short films, assist with outreach, and support our efforts administratively. This web series was founded by filmmakers who wanted a platform where, in the wake of the 2016 election, the many diverse stories of Americans across the country, and abroad, can be shared. In particular, we aim to inspire conversations surrounding topics that are not being told or are not fairly covered by skewed media outlets.
